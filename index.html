About Us

Founded in the heart of the UAE’s construction boom, ADMA Heavy and Light Equipment Rental LLC has grown into one of Dubai’s most reliable and well-equipped partners for infrastructure development. For over 9 years, we’ve played a key role in powering the nation’s public and private sector projects by providing cutting-edge construction equipment and ready-mix concrete services.

From our base in Deira, Dubai, we manage an extensive fleet that includes cranes, bulldozers, concrete pumps, trailers, and mixers — all maintained to the highest safety and performance standards. ADMA doesn’t just rent machines; we deliver peace of mind, reliability, and logistical efficiency to project managers across the UAE.

Our proud track record includes supporting 230+ contractors, completing over 2.8 million cubic meters of concrete projects in Al Ain and 2.3 million in Sharjah, and servicing dozens of large-scale construction sites across Dubai. Whether it’s a major commercial skyscraper, a residential complex, or a private villa foundation, our specialized fleet and technical team are ready to handle the most demanding job requirements.

ADMA is more than a rental service — we are a strategic partner. Our clients return not only for our competitive pricing and specialized fleet, but also for our customer-first approach, responsive support, and deep understanding of project timelines and quality control. As the UAE continues to build its future, we remain committed to scaling alongside it — growing our fleet, refining our services, and remaining at the forefront of innovation in concrete logistics and equipment rental.

Our Equipment

We operate a massive fleet of heavy and light machinery including cranes, bulldozers, concrete pumps (32m to 63m), mixers, trailers, and more. We ensure every unit is serviced and optimized before delivery. Our equipment adapts to both major infrastructure builds and precise residential jobs.

Concrete Works

Concrete is the backbone of every modern structure, and at ADMA, we take pride in delivering it with precision, power, and performance. Our concrete services are powered by a fleet of state-of-the-art pumps ranging from compact line pumps to large-scale boom systems reaching up to 63 meters. These allow us to reach inaccessible locations and reduce manual labor, ensuring cleaner, faster, and more accurate pours.

We offer ready-mix solutions tailored to each project’s specifications. Whether it’s a multi-storey commercial tower, a tight urban residential build, or infrastructure like roads and parking structures, our concrete works are executed with a strict adherence to deadlines and technical requirements. Our experienced crew, logistical support, and pump operators ensure safe operation and smooth coordination on every site.
